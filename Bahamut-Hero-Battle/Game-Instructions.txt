🔥 遊戲名稱：巴哈姆特勇者大亂鬥 🔥

Game Title: Bahamut Hero Grand Battle

⚔️ 遊戲背景故事 / Game Background Story

🌏 在遙遠的數位世界，巴哈姆特的勇者們被困在無止境的通知風暴中！💥 每位英雄都必須在這波瀾壯闊的提醒海洋中生存，並透過獲得通知來增強自己的力量。⚡ 然而，能力越強，移動速度卻會越慢！🐌 你能掌握速度與力量之間的完美平衡，在這場混亂的戰場上勝出嗎？🏆

🌐 In a distant digital realm, the brave adventurers of Bahamut find themselves trapped in an endless storm of notifications! ⚡ Each hero must navigate through the waves of alerts, growing stronger with every notification they receive. However, with great power comes great responsibility—too many notifications will slow you down! 🐢 Can you master the balance of speed and strength to emerge victorious in this chaotic battlefield? 🏅

🕹️ 遊戲玩法 / Gameplay Instructions

🖱️ 操作方式 / Control：使用滑鼠按住（或手機手指按住）來持續移動。

Use your mouse (click and hold) or your finger (press and hold on mobile) to move continuously.

🔔 通知與力量 / Notifications & Power：獲得越多通知分數，移動速度越慢，但攻擊力會變強！💪

The more notification points you collect, the slower your movement becomes, but your attack power increases!

🎯 策略 / Strategy：掌握速度與傷害的平衡，挑戰最高分！🏆

Find the perfect balance between speed and power to achieve the highest score!
