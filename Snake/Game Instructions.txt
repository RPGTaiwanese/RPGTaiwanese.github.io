遊戲說明 / Game Instructions
這是一款進階版的貪食蛇遊戲，除了要吃能量來讓自己變長，還要躲避敵人的彈幕攻擊！
貪食蛇會自動發射彈幕攻擊最近的敵人，並且會隨著分數的提升增加難度！

🎮 遊戲操作 / Controls
滑鼠點擊 / 手機點擊：貪食蛇會朝點擊的方向移動
自動攻擊：貪食蛇會自動發射彈幕攻擊最近的敵人
🆚 敵人類型 / Enemy Types
🟠 橘色敵人（基本敵人） / Orange Enemy (Basic Enemy)
隨機移動並發射彈幕攻擊
碰撞到貪食蛇會讓貪食蛇減少一節身體
🔵 藍色敵人（追蹤敵人） / Blue Enemy (Chasing Enemy)
會主動追蹤貪食蛇的移動
數量有限制，同時最多只會出現 3 隻
🌈 彩色敵人（衝刺敵人） / Rainbow Enemy (Dash Enemy)
只會在 分數超過 100 時出現
會短暫停頓後，快速衝刺向貪食蛇的位置，攻擊更加致命
💥 受傷與死亡 / Damage & Game Over
每節貪食蛇的身體有 10 點生命值
被敵人彈幕擊中：減少 0.1 分數（分數顯示整數），分數每減 1，就會少一節身體
撞到敵人：貪食蛇會直接減少 1 節身體
當貪食蛇身體完全消失時，遊戲結束！
🏆 排行榜 / Leaderboard
遊戲結束後，您可以輸入名稱並將分數存入 Firebase 排行榜
可以查詢特定玩家的分數，看看誰是最強的貪食蛇高手！
現在開始遊戲，看看你能獲得多少分數吧！ 🎯🐍💥

Game Instructions (English)
This is an advanced version of the classic Snake game!
In addition to eating energy to grow, you must dodge enemy bullet attacks!
The snake will automatically fire bullets at the nearest enemy, and the game will become more difficult as your score increases!

🎮 Controls
Mouse Click / Tap on Mobile: The snake will move toward the clicked/tapped location
Auto Attack: The snake automatically shoots bullets at the nearest enemy
🆚 Enemy Types
🟠 Orange Enemy (Basic Enemy)
Moves randomly and shoots bullets at the snake
Colliding with it will make the snake lose one body segment
🔵 Blue Enemy (Chasing Enemy)
Actively follows the snake's movement
Maximum of 3 blue enemies can exist at the same time
🌈 Rainbow Enemy (Dash Enemy)
Appears only when your score exceeds 100
Stops for a moment and then dashes toward the snake’s position, making it more dangerous
💥 Damage & Game Over
Each segment of the snake has 10 HP
Hit by enemy bullets: Lose 0.1 score (only whole numbers are displayed). Every 1 score lost removes 1 body segment
Colliding with an enemy: The snake loses 1 body segment instantly
Game Over when the snake's body disappears!
🏆 Leaderboard
After the game ends, enter your name to save your score to the Firebase leaderboard
You can search for specific players to see who is the best Snake master!
Now, start the game and see how high you can score! 🎯🐍💥
